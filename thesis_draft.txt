\section{Overview of the Architecture}
To overcome the limitations of global functional analysis, this chapter introduces the proposed Melody-Centered Music Structure Analysis (MC-MSA) framework. The core objective of this architecture is to translate raw, polyphonic audio signals into a discrete, analytically meaningful sequence of formal melodic functions.
